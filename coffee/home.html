    About the origin of coffee

    One popular story about the discovery of coffee long ago is about Kaldi, a keeper of goats Kaldi was taking care of his goats in the highlands of Ethiopia where coffee trees have grown for centuries. He noticed that his goats became very excited and active after eating small fruits from a tree. Kaldi reported this discovery to a group of religious workers. When they made a drink out of the fruit, the religious workers realized they could stay awake for long hours of prayer. This knowledge about coffee soon spread all over the world.

For people prone to anxiety disorder, caffeine can worsen symptoms like sweaty palms, heart palpitations, and ringing in the ears.

2.Aggravating hypertension. Caffeine is often combined with other simple analgesics because of its analgesic properties, but long-term use of large amounts of caffeine can only make your condition worse if you already have high blood pressure. Because caffeine alone can raise blood pressure, which, when combined with stress, can multiply dangerously, people at risk for high blood pressure should especially avoid caffeinated beverages during stressful times at work. Some long-time coffee drinkers may think they are immune to the effects of caffeine, but this is not the case, with one study showing that blood pressure can rise for up to 12 hours after drinking a cup of coffee.
