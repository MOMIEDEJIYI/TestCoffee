Music

1
《The Rose》
Singer:Mika Nakashima

If you enjoy a more laid-back, romantic atmosphere, Nagahama's "The Rose" is always delicious. The pure female voice and the delicate babble are perfectly combined with the quiet piano accompaniment. Put down your terrible chores and worries, lie quietly on the sofa, gently sway to the melody, enjoy the music, enjoy the good coffee time......

2
《Pedalo》
Singer:The Heart Strings

"Ballet on the piano" is the best way to describe it. Dancing ballet on the piano, but also exudes classical fragrance. The band from London delighted me with their simple music and friendly sound. The beautiful music and nostalgic classical tone will let you put down your troubles and dance with coffee.

3
《The moon song》
Singer:Karen Orzolek

On The night of The 86th Academy Awards, Karen Orzolek of The Yeah Yeah Yeahs sang "The Moon Song" with Guitar accompaniment by Vampire Weekend's Ezra Koenig. The moon rose behind them, beautiful and romantic.

Ofcourse you can ask for songs that fit the mood.
